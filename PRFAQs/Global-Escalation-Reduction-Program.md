# Press Release: Amazon Digital Operations Reduce Escalations by 20%

**Hyderabad, India —** Amazon Digital and Device Promotions today announced a successful global operations initiative that reduced customer and internal escalations by 20% across EU marketplaces.

The program introduced automated workflows, governance checkpoints, and real-time KPI visibility, improving operational stability while maintaining 100% on-time delivery.

“By redesigning how we detect and resolve risks early, we improved quality while reducing operational noise,” said a Program Manager supporting EU business operations.

The initiative delivered enhanced stakeholder confidence, reduced rework, and enabled teams to focus on strategic growth initiatives.

## FAQs

**Q: What problem did this solve?**  
High escalation volumes were impacting delivery timelines and leadership bandwidth.

**Q: What was done differently?**  
- Introduced workflow automation  
- Defined escalation governance models  
- Implemented KPI dashboards for early risk detection

**Q: What was the measurable impact?**  
- 20% reduction in escalation volume  
- Improved operational predictability  
- Higher stakeholder satisfaction

**Q: How was success measured?**  
Escalation rates, delivery adherence, and leadership feedback.

**Q: What risks were mitigated?**  
Change resistance and cross-team dependencies were mitigated through phased rollouts and leadership alignment.
